SaikouCon

SaikouCon, which means "best convention", is the large scale, annual anime convention of the Pocono Mountains. The convention will always feature some of the best live entertainment themed to Japanese culture as well as the best in J-Rock and J-Pop musical acts from all over the US and beyond. All of this plus top creators, writers, artists, distributors, industry insiders, English dub and original Japanese voice talent, some of the best cosplay talent, shows, masquerades; and so much more!

"If you love anime, you'll love SaikouCon, and SaikouCon will love you."

Save The Date: August 26 - 28, 2016

The Pennsylvania Blues Festival

The Pennsylvania Blues Festival is an award-winning festival celebrating over 20 outstanding years of the Blues in the Poconos. Join us for two days of performances from international and national artists. Musicians perform on two stages on the grounds of Split Rock Resort with evening concerts held inside the spacious Galleria Conference Center, within walking distance of the festival.

Save The Date: September 16 – 18, 2016
