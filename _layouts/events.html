Upcoming Events and Entertainment

From live music to Pennsylvania’s most popular wine and food festival, Split Rock Resort continues to host some of the most anticipated events in the Poconos. Our signature events, including the Great Tastes of Pennsylvania Wine & Food Festival, are held annually on the shores of Lake Harmony. Book your stay with us, and you will have convenient access to all of our fun events.

Don't forget to check out the weekly Activity Schedule or pick up a copy in the lobby. From board games and bingo to dodgeball and turtle racing, there's always something fun going on at Split Rock Resort.

Annual Great Tastes of Pennsylvania Wine Festival

The Great Tastes of Pennsylvania Wine & Food Festival is held annually on resort grounds in June. This two-day, outdoor festival features musical entertainment, many of Pennsylvania's finest wineries, and a wide selection of food and craft vendors. Educational seminars are held during festival hours. Don’t miss all the fantastic new things happening each year as we celebrate some of the best things in life!

Save The Date: June 25th & 26th, 2016

View More Info


The Pennsylvania Blues Festival

The Pennsylvania Blues Festival is an award-winning festival celebrating over 20 outstanding years of the Blues in the Poconos. Join us for two days of performances from international and national artists. Musicians perform on two stages on the grounds of Split Rock Resort with evening concerts held inside the spacious Galleria Conference Center, within walking distance of the festival.

Save The Date: September 16 – 18, 2016
