Resort Overview

You don’t have to go very far to feel like you’re a world away. Situated on over 1,200 acres of forest and mountains, Split Rock Resort is a beautiful escape located only 4 miles from Exit 277 off Interstate 80 and exit 95 of the NE Extension of the Pennsylvania Turnpike.

Our resort offers suites, lodge rooms, villas, and cottages with distinctive mountain town charm. Our lodge rooms, part of the historic Split Rock Lodge, overlook expansive Lake Harmony. Our Willowbrook and Galleria suites are elegant and cozy, while our Villas at Westwood are perfect for long stays, featuring a fireplace, full kitchen, and dining area. Or choose to make the Cottage Duplexes your home away from home for an intimate stay.

A Four-Season Destination

Famous for its natural beauty and outdoor activities, the Poconos Mountains is a destination for adventurers and relaxation-seekers alike. Our resort is not only surrounded by these gorgeous mountains and glistening streams, but it also boasts a location on the pristine 125 acre Lake Harmony, perfect for fishing, boating, and swimming or simply taking in the fresh air.

If you’re into exploring, then you’ll enjoy the area’s excellent nearby hiking, skiing, and snowboarding. But you don’t have to leave the resort to have fun. You’ll love all the things to do on-site. Our indoor/outdoor pool, sandy beach, golf course, massage center, movie theater, and bowling alley are just some of the things to do only at Split Rock! Our large indoor water park, H20ooohh! is a fun way to spend your days no matter the season! During your stay you’ll also have access to the fitness center, hair salon, and a variety of restaurants.

Family-Friendly Activities

Split Rock Resort offers legendary activity programs designed especially for resort guests of all ages and interests. Our talented social staff will lead you through the engaging, educational, and "can't miss" activities, updated weekly, ranging from sporting tournaments, group games, bingo, happy hours, karaoke, turtle racing and more! Download this week's Activity Schedule or pick up a copy in the lobby.

All Hands On Deck

Our beachfront deck is the perfect place for activities and relaxation. Situated on beautiful Lake Harmony, our lagoon features volleyball, a diving board, and sand castle building. Rent boats and tubes, paddleboats, kayaks, and canoes or, on warms days, you’ll find a game of bingo or Frisbee to join. For the ultimate relaxation, sit back and catch some sun in a beach chair and grab a drink from our Sand Bar, Lake Harmony’s only bar on the beach!
