Love In The Poconos

Do you dream of a picturesque mountain backdrop for your wedding day? There is no setting more romantic than the Poconos Mountains and Lake Harmony at Split Rock Resort. With the help of our wedding professionals and specialists, all your wedding dreams will come true. From wedding planning to day-of coordination, we’ll assist you with all the details to make your special event tailored to you.

Split Rock Resort offers beautiful wedding rehearsal, ceremony, reception, and cocktail hour options that will have you and your guests swooning. We look forward to accommodating your wishes, whether you prefer an elegant indoor service or breathtaking outdoor ceremony, intimate gathering or large celebration. We’ll design your customized menu, wedding cake, and help you choose décor.

Wedding Packages, Especially For You

Our comprehensive wedding packages, which feature a range of food, beverage and wedding enhancement options for your reception, can be one way to simplify your planning process. We offer everything from plated evening meals to buffet options, as well as lunch and brunch packages to specialty cocktail receptions. Our packages provide you with a professional wedding specialist, Maître D’, a four hour bar or upgraded open bar, wedding reception décor, champagne or sparkling cider toast for you and your guests, and much more!

We’re also happy to offer exclusive perks like complimentary parking, complimentary accommodations for the bridal couple on the evening of their reception, complimentary breakfast for the newlyweds on the morning following your wedding, and special room rates for your guests.
