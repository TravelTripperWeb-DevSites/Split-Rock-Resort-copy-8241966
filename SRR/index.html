LET YOUR SPIRIT RUN FREE

Enjoy four seasons of fun in the stunning western region of the Poconos at Split Rock Resort. Whether you’re seeking a retreat from the everyday or a family adventure, Split Rock Resort is an experience you’ll look forward to year after year. 

You don’t have to go very far to feel like you’re a world away. Situated on over 1,200 acres of forest and mountains, our resort is a beautiful escape located only 4 miles from Exit 277 off Interstate 80 and exit 95 of the NE Extension of the Pennsylvania Turnpike.

Split Rock Resort hosts a variety of on-site events and live entertainment, from local bands to award-winning festivals. There's always something going on at our resort: check out our weekly Activity Schedule or pick up a copy in the lobby.

Exciting Property Improvements Complete

We’ve been working hard here at Split Rock Resort! Come see all of our changes including the new food court, digital theater showing the very best in first-run movies, and two new restaurants. The Split Rock Grill serves the best in traditional family fare. The Lake View Tavern offers handcrafted, modern American cuisine overlooking Lake Harmony. We’ve also completely renovated our main lobby, upgraded our guest rooms in the historic lakeside lodge, and much more! This summer promises to be one to remember. We can’t wait to welcome you back to Split Rock!

LIVE, LAUGH & PLAY IN THE POCONOS

Split Rock Resort offers a variety of suites, lodge rooms, and villas that exude distinctive mountain town charm. Our lodge rooms, part of the historic Split Rock Lodge, overlook expansive Lake Harmony. Our suites are elegant and cozy, while our villas, which feature a fireplace, full kitchen, and dining area, are perfect for long stays.

What makes a vacation at Split Rock Resort a memorable one is our fantastic selection of resort amenities. With access to sporting courts, pools, a fitness center, golf course, salon, variety of dining options, and more, you won’t ever want to leave! Children and adults especially love our H2Oooohh! Indoor Waterpark, with fun for the entire family.

One visit to Split Rock Resort and you’ll be hooked! Guests return every year to the Poconos for the on-site activities, outdoor adventures, and charming accommodations.

H20ooohh! Indoor Water Park

Adjacent to the Galleria at Split Rock Resort, this indoor water park features attractions for all ages, including the Komodo Dragon – Pennsylvania’s First Indoor Flow Rider! Thrill-seekers will enjoy The Viper, Piranha and Amazon Blast slides which drop an incredible four stories. Kids will have a great time at the Jungle Falls & Leapin' Lizards play areas. Open to the public.
