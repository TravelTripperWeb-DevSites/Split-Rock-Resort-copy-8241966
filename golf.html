A Golfing Experience

Golfing in the Poconos just got even better. Discover your best game at the Split Rock Golf Club, our 27-hole championship golf course nestled in the Pocono Mountains. The courseâ€™s unique design and natural terrain makes it challenging for all levels of play. With some of the most spectacular views in the entire Northeast, our course boasts undulating fairways and demanding water hazards.

Our fully trained professionals are always on staff to offer knowledgeable information on everything from your club choice to the latest trends in golfing apparel. Our teaching pros are also available to provide valuable advice and host a number of lessons and clinics for all abilities.

For more information visit: http://www.golfsplitrock.com

Shop the Gear

The Pro Shop at Split Rock Golf Club sports many of the top brands in golf apparel, shoes and accessories for men, women and children. We presently stock Oakley, Nike, Foot Joy Shoes, Ahead, Sun Mt., Slazenger and Antigua, as well as a wide variety of logoed items. For more information about Split Rock Golf Club, visit our full website: http://www.golfsplitrock.com


Membership & Packages

A variety of membership options are available to Split Rock Golf Club for individuals, couples, families and corporations. The Club is devoted to the satisfaction of our members and offers many perks in addition to golf play including driving range balls, lockers, 10% off at Sunset Green Restaurant, 10% off Pro Shop Merchandise (not including sale items), and advance tee times.
