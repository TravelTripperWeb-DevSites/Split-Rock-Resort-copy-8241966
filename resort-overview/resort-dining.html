Resort Dining

Convenient Cuisine At Split Rock Resort

Dining

Indulge your cravings at the delicious restaurants exclusive to Split Rock Resort. From pizza at the H20 Island Oasis to the delicious breakfast spread at Split Rock Grill and traditional American favorites and brews at Thirsty Moose Saloon, we've got something to please every taste. Dining hours of operation are subject to change without notice. Don’t forget to visit our Food Court for coffee, ice cream, pizza, sandwich and more! Click here to check out our pizza and deli menus.*

Split Rock Grill

This is continental dining at its best. Relax in our terraced dining room for delicious breakfast selections, Saturday and Sunday from 7:30am to 11:00am and Monday through Friday 7:30am to 11:00am in the Rock Lounge. Or join us for dinner, Friday and Saturday from 5:00pm to 10:00pm and Sunday through Thursday from 5:00pm to 10:00pm in the Rock Lounge.

Thirsty Moose Saloon

Enjoy stunning views of Lake Harmony from the Thirsty Moose. Enjoy a refreshing brew or sip cocktails while taking in the majestic surroundings and relaxing atmosphere.

The Thirsty Moose Saloon is known for its selection of brews, live entertainment, and daily drink specials. You can even kick back and watch your favorite sports team on one of our many monitors and big screen television. The best spot in the area to enjoy the NFL Sunday Ticket!

H2O Island Oasis

Famished after a few too many spins on The Viper slide? We’ve got your hunger covered! Enjoy a savory bite, from pizza and burgers to funnel cake fries. Our cabana themed island bar offers delicious frozen margaritas, piña coladas, and more tasty beverages. H2O Island Oasis is open during water park hours.
